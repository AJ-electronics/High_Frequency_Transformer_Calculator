High Frequency Ferrite Transformer Calculator

This calculator assists in designing ferrite transformers operating from 10 kHz to several MHz using ferrite core families such as ETD, EE, Toroidal, RM and PQ. It calculates the minimum required core area product, suggests suitable cores, computes turns, evaluates skin depth and wire requirements.
